About us

We Are A Student Association Empowering Visionary Game Changers At Bocconi

We Love Technology

We have a passion for Technology as it’s the exciting force offering us the widest range of opportunities that any generation has ever experienced before. If not for technology, the way we live would be completely less vivid

We Are On A Mission

We want to contribute proactively and positively to the advancements of our society spreading the technology culture – particularly in our far too slowly moving Italy

We Provide A Stage For Inspiration

Tech@B will be a platform in which brilliant and entrepreneurial minds meet to grow their passion for technology, exchange ideas, empower each other and envision the future together

What we do

We Dream, We Laugh, We Create!

TECH NEWS

Our social network profiles are daily updated with the most interesting tech news from around the globe. Additionally, every week we craft our picks of the #TopTechStories and we share our personal analysis of the most notable tech news

CODING DAYS

We believe in the importance of coding. Thus, we decided to learn the basics of programming practicing together with several exercises in recurring meetings, in order to achieve more and more. We code, we grow and we have fun!

TECH EVENTS

We bring notable Tech speakers in Bocconi and we organize events in collaboration with other associations inside and outside our university

CAREER ADVISORY

We pick the most interesting career opportunities, projects and workshops about technology-related field and help each other is getting our dream job
